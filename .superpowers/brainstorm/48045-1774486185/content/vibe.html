What vibe should your portfolio have?

This sets the tone for everything — colors, fonts, layout, and feel

A

Dark & Neon

Black/dark background with bright accent colors (purple, cyan, green). Edgy, gamer aesthetic. Feels like a high-end Minecraft content creator.

B

Clean & Minimal

White or light background, subtle shadows, bold typography. Professional, easy to read. Lets your work speak for itself.

C

Bold & Creative

Dark with rich colors, gradient accents, dynamic layout. Feels like a creative studio. Eye-catching and memorable.

D

Minecraft-Inspired

Pixelated details, blocky fonts, earthy greens and browns mixed with modern design. Unique and on-brand for your niche.
